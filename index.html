Campfire Cooking in Another World with My Absurd Skill

Unwittingly summoned to another world, Tsuyoshi Mukouda has nothing but the clothes on his back and the ability to order mail from his favorite grocery back in Japan. Now calling himself Mukohda, he finds that this otherworld is teeming with monsters. Denizens of this world fend for themselves with sword and sorcery, but former-salaryman Mukohda can only bring to bear an assortment of wares and ingredients from Earth!

Life isn't easy for Mukohda. Or at least it wouldn't be, had he not met Fel, a legendary fenrir and unimaginably powerful. He is also a glutton. With the promise of Mukohda's Earth-inspired cooking, Fel readily agrees to be his familiar, with a peculiar slime Sui later joining them as familiar (and glutton) number two. And just like that, their lives are that of absolute safety and- more importantly- good eats!
