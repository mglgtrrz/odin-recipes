Wyvern Gyudon

Such an easy yet filling meal, a certain fenrir and slime have no beef ("gyu") with it

A wyvern is a large dragonoid to be feared in the otherworld. Typically seen in groups, a flight of wyverns can all too easily spell the ruin of even a well-defended town. They have knife-sharp teeth. They wield poisonous stingers. They fly. No surprises there, really.

To Fel the fenrir and Sui the (freakishly strong) slime, a flight of wyverns is a convenient way to get large amounts of high-quality marbled meat all at once. For this, Mukohda's Earth cooking repertoire has just the thing! More importantly, it's fast and simple- all the easier to sate his pair of gluttons.

Ingredients

  • 225 grams wyvern meat, either ribeye or chuck steak cuts
  • 120 grams white onions
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup dry sake
  • 2 tbsps soy sauce
  • 12 grams sugar, plus more to taste
  • 1 tsp tubed ginger or roughly 4 grams fresh ginger
  • Freshly cooked rice
  • Pickled red ginger (beni-shoga) to taste
