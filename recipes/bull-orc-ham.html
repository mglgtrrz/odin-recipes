Bloody Horn Bull x Orc General: Hamburger Steak

A staple favorite of a certain voracious fenrir and slime

In this otherworld, Bloody Horn Bull and Orc General meat are top-shelf protein, indeed. Of course, one doesn't simply get their hands on such quality produce. Bloody Horn Bulls grow to the size of a rhino and travel in herds, each one with a violent temperament. The presence of an Orc General, a higher-form variant of the species, means that you're likely to face-off a veritable horde of orcs as a matter of course. Yep, good luck alright.

With Fel (a legendary fenrir) and Sui (an abnormally powerful slime), however, procuring these ingredients is just another light workout. With Mukoda's repertoire of Earth's cuisine and his access to Japan's grocery offerings, what you get is comfort food with a literal twist of fantasy. Unreasonable you say? Bah, it's freaking delicious, so don't think about it too deeply!
